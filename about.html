About Us

TOHA CLASSIC Transport is a family owned transport company that specializes in transportation of passenger bus services. Our commitment to excellence, reliability, and passenger satisfaction has made us a trusted choice for travellers across Bangladesh.Our journey began in 2021 with just 2 buses on the Nilphamari to Noakhali route. Thanks to the sincere love and support from our passengers, we now operate with more than 5 buses. Our main goal is to provide safe, comfortable, and punctual service. We continuously work to improve our service quality to ensure our passengers are always satisfied. Each of our buses is equipped with modern amenities, air-conditioned environments, and skilled and friendly drivers and assistants. We prioritize passenger safety, which is why our buses undergo regular maintenance and safety checks. We have simplified the ticket booking process for our passengers so that they can easily purchase tickets online. Our future plans include adding more routes and increasing the number of buses so that more passengers can enjoy our service. Making your journey pleasant and hassle-free with us is our only goal.
